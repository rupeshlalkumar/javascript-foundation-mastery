## 2. Arithmetic Operators

Learn:

- Addition (`+`)
- Subtraction (`-`)
- Multiplication (`*`)
- Division (`/`)
- Modulus (`%`)
- Exponentiation (`**`)
- Increment (`++`)
- Decrement (`--`)

Business Examples:

- Revenue
- Profit
- Discount
- GST
- Salary
- Average Marks

---

## 3. Comparison Operators

Learn:

- Equal (`==`)
- Strict Equal (`===`)
- Not Equal (`!=`)
- Strict Not Equal (`!==`)
- Greater Than (`>`)
- Less Than (`<`)
- Greater Than or Equal (`>=`)
- Less Than or Equal (`<=`)

Business Examples:

- Login Validation
- Age Verification
- Salary Comparison
- Stock Availability
- Course Eligibility

---

## 4. Logical Operators

Learn:

- AND (`&&`)
- OR (`||`)
- NOT (`!`)

Business Examples:

- User Login
- Admin Access
- Payment Verification
- Form Validation
- CRM Lead Qualification

## Calculator UI Logic

Support:

```text
Addition

Subtraction

Multiplication

Division
```

Example:

```javascript
const number1 = 20;
const number2 = 10;

console.log(number1 + number2);
console.log(number1 - number2);
console.log(number1 * number2);
console.log(number1 / number2);
```
