# 📘 Day 3: JavaScript Operators

Welcome to **Day 3** of the **JavaScript Foundation Mastery** roadmap.

Today, you will learn one of the most fundamental concepts in programming: **JavaScript Operators**.

Operators allow JavaScript to perform calculations, compare values, and make logical decisions. From simple calculators to enterprise-level CRM, LMS, ERP, and E-Commerce systems, operators are used everywhere.

---

# 🎯 Goal

Understand how JavaScript uses operators to:

- Perform Mathematical Calculations
- Compare Values
- Make Logical Decisions
- Build Business Logic

By the end of today, you will be able to design the logic behind calculators and solve real-world business problems using JavaScript operators.

---

# 📚 Topics Covered

## 1. Operators

Understand:

- What are Operators?
- Why Operators Exist
- Types of Operators
- Internal Working
- Business Examples
- Common Mistakes
- Interview Questions

---

## 2. Arithmetic Operators

Learn:

- Addition (`+`)
- Subtraction (`-`)
- Multiplication (`*`)
- Division (`/`)
- Modulus (`%`)
- Exponentiation (`**`)
- Increment (`++`)
- Decrement (`--`)

Business Examples:

- Revenue Calculation
- Profit Calculation
- GST
- Discount
- Salary
- Average Marks

---

## 3. Comparison Operators

Learn:

- Equal (`==`)
- Strict Equal (`===`)
- Not Equal (`!=`)
- Strict Not Equal (`!==`)
- Greater Than (`>`)
- Less Than (`<`)
- Greater Than or Equal (`>=`)
- Less Than or Equal (`<=`)

Business Examples:

- Login Validation
- Student Eligibility
- Product Availability
- Salary Comparison
- Stock Verification

---

## 4. Logical Operators

Learn:

- AND (`&&`)
- OR (`||`)
- NOT (`!`)

Business Examples:

- User Login
- Admin Access
- Payment Verification
- Form Validation
- CRM Lead Qualification
